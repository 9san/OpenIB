Etiquette


  1. This website is an anonymous community. Please refrain from using any identifiers.
  2. Stick to the subject and don't derail threads with offtopic posts or infighting.
  3. Do not spam a thread, pretend to be more than one user, or make a lot of posts in succession.
  4. Bumping threads on first page is completely unnecessary.
  5. Keep low effort content to a minimum (LOL XD, bump, etc).
  6. Use the post features. If you refer to an image or video, make sure to embed it in your posts for other users. Upload a screenshot to preview a link. When replying, quote the comment(s) you're replying to by clicking the post number.
